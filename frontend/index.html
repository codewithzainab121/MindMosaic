What We Offer

Everything you need to
nurture your mind

Built on evidence-based therapeutic frameworks, MindMosaic combines the latest AI with genuine compassion.

📊
Mood Tracking

Log your daily mood with a simple 1-10 score. Visualise trends over time and receive personalised affirmations based on how you feel.

🆘
Crisis Detection

Real-time scanning for distress signals. If crisis language is detected, immediate hotline resources appear — automatically and prominently.

🧘
Guided Exercises

4-7-8 breathing, body scans, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, thought records, and more — each exercise walks you step-by-step at your own pace.

🔒
Private by Default

Conversations stay in your browser session. No account required to start chatting. Your mental health journey belongs to you.

The Process

Simple as breathing

Four steps from opening the app to feeling genuinely supported.

1
Share How You Feel

Type freely. No judgment, no forms — just your words in a safe space.

2
AI Retrieves Context

The RAG system finds relevant therapeutic knowledge to ground the response.

3
LLM Crafts Response

LangChain assembles a compassionate, evidence-based reply via OpenRouter.

4
Track Your Progress

Log mood, complete exercises, and build healthy habits over time.
